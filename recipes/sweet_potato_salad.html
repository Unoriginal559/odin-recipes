Sweet Potato Salad

Description

A simple recipe for sweet potato salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 white potatos
  • 1 sweet potato
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
