Cocktail Meatballs

Description

A cocktail meatball recipe used for parties by the original creator of the recipe. They do well in a slow cooker, so they can be kept hot for the duration of the party.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 8 ounce can jellied cranberry sauce
  • 3/4 cup chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
