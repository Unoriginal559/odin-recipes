Lasagna

Description

A lasgna recipe focused on ease of making. It features a short list of standard ingredients and some seasonings. This recipe should be easy to follow even for the inexperienced. Good for those craving a lasagna without too much effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 32 ounce jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water
